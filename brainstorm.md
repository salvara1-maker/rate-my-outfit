# Brainstorm - Rate My Outfit

## App Idea

Rate My Outfit is a mobile app where users can upload photos of their outfits and receive ratings from others.

## Problem

People often want feedback on their outfits before going out but may not have someone available to ask.

## Solution

This app allows users to quickly upload a photo and receive ratings, helping them feel more confident in their outfit choices.

## Target Users

* College students
* Young adults
* Social media users interested in fashion

## Key Features

* Upload outfit photos using camera
* Rate outfits (1–10 or emoji system)
* View a feed of outfits
* Save and view your past outfits

## Why This App?

This app is fun, interactive, and encourages daily engagement. It uses mobile features like the camera, making it more than just a basic website.
